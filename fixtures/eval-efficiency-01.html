# Acme Robotics R1 — Technical Specifications

The Acme R1 is a mid-size mobile manipulation platform intended for light industrial and research use.

**Key specifications:**

- Dimensions: 82 cm x 54 cm x 110 cm (stowed)
- Mass: 38 kg
- Maximum payload (arm): 5 kg
- Maximum runtime on a single charge: 6 hours
- Operating temperature: 0°C to 40°C
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Ethernet, optional 5G
- SDK: Python and C++ bindings, ROS 2 Humble compatible

The R1 ships with a six-degree-of-freedom arm, a parallel-jaw gripper (swappable), a 2D LIDAR, and stereo RGB-D cameras for navigation and manipulation.
