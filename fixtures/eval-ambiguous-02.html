# Perseverance Rover

Perseverance is a NASA Mars rover. It is the successor to Curiosity and shares much of its chassis. Its mission focuses on astrobiology, including the search for signs of ancient microbial life, and it is caching samples for a planned future return to Earth.

Perseverance carried a small technology demonstrator called Ingenuity, a helicopter that completed the first powered flight on another planet. Ingenuity far exceeded its planned flight count.
